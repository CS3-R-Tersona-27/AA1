Spell creation is an integral part of Deepwoken and its combat. Spells are meant to be completely unique to their user, as players are given the ability to mess with and create spells with the game’s healthy customizations. Spells themselves can also have lore significance, as the games lore is based on real actions done by players themselves, and just like in reality, if something significant happens, many know about it, and if something significant happens in Deepwoken, it will become tied into Deepwoken’s lore. Lets say, a person uses an exceedingly powerful spell, such as a fire spell spawning a meteor, and uses it to destroy thousands of villages ingame. Eventually, this will be known to many, and this spell or user will become infamous for these actions. Spells have increasingly important aspects in the game, not just in combat, but you cannot have spells without first creating a spell.
